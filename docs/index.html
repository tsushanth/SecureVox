Frequently Asked Questions

How does offline transcription work?

SecureVox uses OpenAI's Whisper model, optimized to run on Apple's Neural Engine. The AI model is downloaded once and runs entirely on your iPhone - no internet needed for transcription.

Which iPhones are supported?

SecureVox works on iPhone 12 and later with iOS 17+. Older iPhones can still use Apple's built-in speech recognition as a fallback.

How accurate is the transcription?

Whisper is one of the most accurate speech recognition models available. It handles accents, background noise, and multiple languages remarkably well.

Is there a recording time limit?

No artificial limits. Record as long as you need - from quick 10-second notes to hour-long meetings.

Can I import existing audio files?

Yes! Import MP3, M4A, WAV, and other audio formats. Great for transcribing podcasts, interviews, or recordings from other apps.

Is my data backed up?

Your recordings are stored locally and included in your iPhone's iCloud backup (if enabled). We never have access to your data.
