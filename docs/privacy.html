Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2025

SecureVox is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our voice recording and transcription app handles your data.

Data Collection and Storage

SecureVox is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All data stays on your device. Your voice recordings, transcriptions, and settings are stored locally on your device only.
  • No cloud uploads. We do not upload your audio files or transcriptions to any server.
  • No account required. You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.
  • On-device processing. All transcription is performed locally using on-device AI (Whisper), ensuring your conversations remain private.

Permissions

SecureVox requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required to record voice memos. Audio is only recorded when you explicitly tap the record button, and recordings are stored locally on your device.
  • Storage: Used to save your recordings and transcriptions to your device's local storage.
  • Notifications: Used to show transcription progress when the app is in the background.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Your recordings and transcriptions never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them using your device's share functionality.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. When you delete a recording within the app, it is permanently removed from your device. Uninstalling the app will also remove all associated data.

Children's Privacy

SecureVox does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
